Process

Dream big, test small, scale incrementally, learn, improve. My process is largely influenced by the founding principles of agile and human-centered design. Here are the core pillars of my process:


Think Holistic and Human-Centered

Deeply understand the needs of all stakeholders impacted by your system. Only when you understand the needs of not just your users, but also your staff as well as others impacted farther downstream, will you be able to implement a sustainable solution.



Always Test Before Implementing

Implementation is expensive regardless of the medium. Before implementing, always use cost effective pilots and prototypes to gauge feedback before scaling.



Dream, Test, Scale, Evaluate, Repeat!

Dream big, but implement small. When you are ready to implement something new, break a small piece off and test it before implmenting. Once you think you have it right, scale it. Finally, test it. Understand if it did what you thought it would. If not, learn from it and use that to inform your next delivery. This approach will ensure costs and waste are kept to an absolute minimum.
