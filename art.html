Art


Overview

Art has been important to me throughout my life and is ultimately what taught me how to bring an idea to life. I therefore decided to include a few projects.

Projects:


Digital Art

The majority of the digital work that I have done throughout my career has been done as an intermediary step for bringing something else to life. This work however was different as it was in itself the final product and the manifestation of an exploration into using digital tools to create abstract forms.



Street Studies

I love cities. I'm particularly fascinated by how they orchestrate human behavior. I therefore have spent many hours documenting them through a variety of techniques.



Sculpture

Scuplture was arguably the first step in my journey to become who I am today. I remember the first time I sketched something on paper, and then brought it to life with clay. That experience was what gave me the confidence to move down this path and become who I am today.
